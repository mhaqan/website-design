Repair Service Page

All our bike repair work has a fixed pricing structure and we offer the following services across all types of bikes including mountain bikes, kids bikes & electric bikes. Tyres and Wheels Puncture Repair - tyre and/or tube replacement Wheel spindle/bearing replacement/re-grease Wheel truing (subject to wheel condition) Wheel assembly (with tyre and tube) and fitting Wheel build/spoke replacement Slime puncture-proof service Gears Gear adjustment Gear service (including replacement inner cables) Gear cable - replace and adjust Chain fitting Front/rear mech fitting Bottom bracket service or replacement Crank set replacement Brakes Brake adjustment (with existing cable) Brake cable replacement and adjustment Brake service - cable type (including replacement inner cables) Brake service - hydraulic disc (replace fluid and bleed system) Brake block fitting and adjustment (front or rear) Fitting new free-wheel or cassette (wheel only) Steering and suspension Headset or fork replacement Headset service Handlebar replacement Suspension fork fitting (free if purchased from Halfords)
